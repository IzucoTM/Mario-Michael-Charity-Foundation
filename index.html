ABOUT US

Who We Are? Mario Michael Charity Foundation (Gboo Nti Nation 🌎) is a nonprofit organization committed to transforming lives and uplifting underserved communities across Nigeria and beyond. We believe that every individual deserves access to basic needs — food, education, healthcare, and opportunity. Our mission is to be a source of hope and positive change for those who need it most. Through compassion, transparency, and action, we are building a nation of impact — one life at a time.

OUR MISSION

To empower disadvantaged individuals and communities by providing sustainable support in education, healthcare, food security, and youth development.

OUR VISION

To create a world where no one is left behind — a world where compassion drives change and communities thrive together.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • • Compassion - We serve with love and empathy.
  • • Integrity - Transparency and accountability in everything we do.
  • • Impact - We focus on measurable and lasting change.
  • • Unity - Together, we are stronger.
  • • Service - We exist to uplift others.
